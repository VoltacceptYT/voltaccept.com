Imperial Alternatives

Imperial Alternatives is a mod I created for both Skyrim SE and Skyrim VR, designed to add new content to the game, including two new cloak variants: the Imperial Soldier Cloak and the Imperial Legate Cloak. These cloaks, along with other features, bring additional depth to the province of Skyrim. To craft the cloaks, you’ll need 2 Leather and 3 Leather Strips. They can be crafted in Solitude, Falkreath, Morthal, and Markarth. After completing the Battle for Whiterun and siding with the Empire in the Civil War, you'll also be able to craft them in Whiterun.
Required DLCs: Dawnguard
